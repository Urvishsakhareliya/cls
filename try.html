Product Description

We are offering Wildcraft HYPA SHIELD W95 Reusable Protection Mask to our clients.

Features and Benefits:
  • Comfortable breathing 
  • Wider face coverage for maximum protection
  • Innovative 2-panel design for universal fit 
  • Adjustable nose clip for enhanced protection
  • Soft, elastic ear loops for extra comfort 
  • Minimal eyewear fogging 
  • Low heat build up
  • Convenient flat-fold design for easy storage 
  • Washable and reusable 
  • Value for money
  • Accommodates all facial movements

HYPASHIELD 6-Layer Triple Particle Filtration System:

Inner Layer
  • Moisture Control
  • Anti-bacterial Finish

Melt Blown (2 Layer)
  • Bacteria Filter
  • Small Particle Filter

Spun Bonded (2 Layer)
  • Droplet Protection

Outer Layer
  • Coarse Particle Filter

Instructions for Use:
  1. Hold ear strings by your hands, face the concave side
  2. Pull the strings over your ears, cover your nose, mouth & chin with the mask properly
  3. Adjust the bendable 'nose clip' to avoid leakage and to ensure the perfect fit
  4. Inner layer should be pushed in to make the concave shape; it should not be pulled out

Precautions
  • Change the mask if there is difficulty in breathing
  • Store the mask in a dry place and away from sunlight
  • Wash after everyday use 
  • For individual use, not to be shared
  • Mask needs to be discarded after 30 washes
  • Wash your hands properly before wearing and removing the mask

    Warning
    • Usage instructions & precautions should be followed as recommended
    • Improper usage of the mask may cause health risks

    Wash Care:
    • Dip and soak
    • Do not bleach
    • Do not tumble dry
    • Do not iron
    • Do not dry clean
    • Do not wring
    • Drip dry
